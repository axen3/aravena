Shipping Information

Free Shipping Across Morocco

We deliver to all 12 regions and over 1,000 cities & villages – completely FREE!

Delivery Time

  • Casablanca, Rabat, Marrakech, Agadir, Tangier, Fes: 2–4 business days
  • Other cities: 3–6 business days
  • Remote areas & villages: 4–8 business days

How It Works

1. Place your order → 2. We pack it carefully → 3. Partner courier delivers to your door → 4. Pay the courier in cash when you receive your package

Tracking Your Order

Once shipped, you’ll receive an SMS with the tracking number and courier name (Amana, CTM Messagerie, or local partner).

We don’t ship to P.O. boxes. Please provide a full physical address with clear landmark.
