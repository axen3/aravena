Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to Aravena Store. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, especially regarding our Cash on Delivery service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum required information necessary to fulfill your order and provide customer service:

  • Personal Identification: Your Full Name and Phone Number.
  • Delivery Details: Your Full Address and City (for shipment purposes).
  • Order Details: Information about the products you purchased.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To process, confirm, and deliver your order via our courier partners.
  • To contact you via phone or WhatsApp regarding your order confirmation or delivery status.
  • To process returns and refunds, if necessary.
  • To improve our website and service offerings (in an aggregated, anonymized way).

3. Data Sharing and Protection

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • **Courier Partners:** We share your Name, Phone Number, and Address with our trusted courier service providers for the sole purpose of delivering your order.
  • **Legal Requirements:** We may release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law.

Your data is stored securely and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights and are required to keep the information confidential.

4. Cash on Delivery (COD)

Since we only process Cash on Delivery (COD) payments, we **do not collect or store any of your financial or payment card details.** All financial transactions happen directly between you and the courier agent at the time of delivery.

5. Your Consent

By using our site and submitting your order details, you consent to our privacy policy. If we make changes to our policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date on this page.
