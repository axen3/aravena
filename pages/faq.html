Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Shopping is easy! Simply browse our products, add your desired items to the cart, and proceed to the checkout page. Fill in your name, phone number, address, and city. Since we only offer Cash on Delivery (COD), no payment information is needed upfront. Click "Confirm Order," and we will call you to confirm the order before shipping.

We offer **FREE SHIPPING** on all orders across Morocco. Delivery typically takes **3 to 7 business days** depending on your city. We work with reliable local couriers to ensure fast and safe delivery right to your door.

We accept **Cash on Delivery (COD)** only. You pay the full amount in cash directly to the delivery person once you receive and inspect your order. This makes shopping safe and secure for everyone.

We offer a **30-Day Easy Return Policy**. If you are not 100% satisfied, you can contact us via WhatsApp within 30 days of receiving your order, and we will arrange a free return pickup. Once the item is received and checked, we issue a full refund via bank transfer.

Once your order is confirmed and shipped, you will receive a tracking number via WhatsApp or SMS. You can use this number on our partner courier's website to monitor the status of your delivery.
